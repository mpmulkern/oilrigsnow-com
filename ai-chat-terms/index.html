AI Chat Terms & Disclosure

Effective Date: May 22, 2026  •  Last Updated: May 22, 2026

🤖 You are interacting with an AI assistant, not a human representative.

The chat widget on this website is powered by an artificial intelligence (AI) assistant. This page discloses what the AI can and cannot do, how your conversation data is handled, and how to reach a real person at Oil Rigs Now.

1. What Is the AI Chat Assistant?

Oil Rigs Now uses an AI-powered chat assistant on our website to help visitors find equipment information, answer common questions, and connect with our team. The assistant is a software system trained to respond to questions about oilfield equipment and the Oil Rigs Now inventory.

The chat assistant operates automatically and does not involve a human Oil Rigs Now employee unless you are explicitly transferred to one. It is visible as a floating chat icon in the lower-right corner of the page and displays an AI indicator label so you always know you are communicating with an automated system.

2. What the AI Assistant Knows

The AI assistant is designed to help with:

  • Information about drilling rigs and oilfield equipment generally (types, specifications, industry terminology)
  • The Oil Rigs Now equipment inventory listed on this website
  • General information about how to request a quote or contact our team
  • Navigation and use of the Oil Rigs Now website

The assistant's knowledge is based on information available at the time it was configured. Inventory and pricing information may not reflect real-time availability.

3. Limitations of the AI Assistant

The AI assistant is a convenience tool only. It does not provide professional advice, legal guidance, engineering assessments, or binding commercial commitments of any kind.

Specifically, the AI assistant:

  • Cannot confirm real-time equipment availability — availability changes frequently; contact us directly to confirm
  • Cannot provide binding pricing — all price information from the AI is indicative only; a formal written quotation from Oil Rigs Now is required
  • Cannot commit to any contract, sale, or agreement — no statement made by the AI constitutes an offer, acceptance, or binding commitment
  • Cannot provide engineering or technical certification — always engage qualified oilfield engineers for technical decisions
  • May make errors or provide outdated information — AI systems can produce incorrect responses; verify important information directly with our team
  • Does not replace human judgment — purchasing decisions involving significant capital should involve direct consultation with our experienced team

Oil Rigs Now is not liable for any decisions made in reliance on AI chat responses.

4. Conversation Data & Privacy

When you use the chat assistant, your conversation content is collected and processed. Here is how that data is handled:

  • Collection: The content of your messages and the AI's responses are logged.
  • Purpose: Logs are used to improve the quality of the AI assistant, investigate complaints, and ensure the assistant operates correctly.
  • Storage: Conversation logs may be retained for up to 12 months.
  • Third-party processing: Conversations are processed by our AI service provider under a data processing agreement. The provider is not authorized to use your conversation data for its own purposes.
  • No sensitive data: Do not share sensitive personal information, financial account details, passwords, export-controlled technical data, or confidential business information in the chat.
  • Access and deletion: You may request access to or deletion of your conversation data by contacting us at michael@oilrigsnow.com.

For full privacy details, see our Privacy Policy. For cookies set by the chat widget, see our Cookie Policy.

5. Reaching a Human Representative

If you need to speak with a real person at Oil Rigs Now at any time, you can:

  • Ask the chat assistant to "connect me to a human" or "transfer to a person"
  • Call us directly at 713-565-0747
  • Email us at info@oilrigsnow.com
  • Submit a contact form at our Contact page

Our team is based in Houston, TX (Central Time). We respond to all serious equipment inquiries within one business day.

6. Acceptable Use of the Chat

You agree not to use the AI chat assistant to:

  • Submit harmful, abusive, or threatening content
  • Attempt to manipulate the AI into providing false, misleading, or harmful outputs
  • Extract or reproduce proprietary Oil Rigs Now business information for competitive purposes
  • Submit content that violates any applicable laws

We reserve the right to terminate chat sessions and block users who misuse the chat feature.

7. Changes to This Disclosure

We may update this disclosure when we change AI providers, update the assistant's capabilities, or change how conversation data is handled. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date on this page.
