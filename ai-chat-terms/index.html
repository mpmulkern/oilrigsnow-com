1. What Is the AI Chat

The AI chat assistant on oilrigsnow.com appears as a floating widget in the bottom-right corner of the screen. It is powered by an artificial intelligence language model and is designed to answer questions about oilfield equipment, our inventory, and the equipment brokerage process.

The AI chat assistant is not a human employee of Oil Rigs Now, LLC. It is an automated system that generates responses based on its training data and the information it has been provided about our company and inventory. While it can provide helpful information quickly, its responses should be understood within the limitations described in this document.

Plain English: The chat widget is a computer program, not a person. It can answer many common questions well, but it can make mistakes and cannot make commitments on behalf of our company.

2. Visual Indicator

The AI chat interface displays a clear AI label to indicate that you are interacting with an automated assistant, not a human representative. This label is visible throughout the chat session.

If you experience an unusual response delay — for example, the assistant taking several seconds to reply — this is a normal characteristic of AI language model processing and does not indicate that a human has joined the conversation. Our human representatives do not monitor the AI chat in real time. For time-sensitive matters or complex transactions, please contact us directly as described in Section 7.

3. What the AI Knows

The AI chat assistant has been provided with information about our company and inventory and is designed to assist with the following topics:

  • Oil Rigs Now equipment inventory — land rigs, mobile rigs, offshore equipment, and workover units
  • General oilfield equipment terminology, component descriptions, and industry standards
  • Drilling rig configurations, mast and derrick types, drawworks, mud systems, and BOP equipment
  • How to read and interpret equipment specifications and HP ratings
  • Company contact information, office hours, and how to submit an RFQ (Request for Quote)
  • How the buying and selling process works at Oil Rigs Now
  • General information about the oilfield equipment market and common buyer questions
  • Directing you to the right department or contact for specific inquiries

The AI assistant is regularly updated with current inventory information. However, real-time availability cannot be guaranteed — always confirm current status with a human representative before commencing due diligence.

4. What the AI Does NOT Do

The following are outside the scope of the AI chat assistant. The AI will attempt to redirect you to appropriate human resources for these matters:

  • Confirm real-time equipment availability (markets move; only human representatives can confirm)
  • Provide binding price quotes or commit to any price
  • Create, modify, or sign contracts or any legally binding commitments
  • Provide engineering, structural, or safety assessments of any equipment
  • Replace professional inspection or third-party due diligence
  • Provide legal, financial, tax, or investment advice
  • Access or disclose private deal information, NDA-protected data, or confidential client information
  • Process payments or initiate financial transactions
  • Guarantee shipping timelines, logistics arrangements, or export documentation
  • Make representations about equipment condition that substitute for physical inspection

If the AI chat provides any information touching on these areas, treat that information as illustrative only and verify directly with an authorized ORN representative before acting on it.

5. No Binding Commitments

Any price, specification, availability status, timeline, or other commercial term mentioned in the AI chat is provided for informational purposes only. No statement made by the AI chat assistant — whether about equipment price, condition, availability, financing, or logistics — constitutes a binding offer, acceptance, or commitment of any kind by Oil Rigs Now, LLC.

All commitments, offers, and agreements require written confirmation from an authorized ORN human representative. This applies to price, availability, condition, timeline, and all other material terms.

"If it's not in writing from a human, it's not a commitment."

Oil Rigs Now, LLC — Transaction Policy

Submitting an RFQ through the website or via email initiates a process that will be handled by a human representative. Any figures or terms discussed in AI chat prior to that process are not binding on either party.

6. Data Handling

Conversations with the AI chat assistant may be logged and stored. By using the chat, you consent to the following data practices:

  • Logging: Chat transcripts may be retained by Oil Rigs Now and/or our AI service provider for quality assurance, accuracy improvement, and compliance purposes.
  • Human review: ORN staff may review chat transcripts to assess the quality of AI responses, identify errors, and improve the assistant's training data.
  • Retention: Chat logs may be retained for up to 24 months.
  • Third-party processing: Transcripts may be processed by our AI service provider under a data processing agreement consistent with our Privacy Policy.

Do not share confidential information in the AI chat. Do not enter confidential business information, financial data, proprietary technical specifications, personal identification numbers, banking details, or information protected by a non-disclosure agreement. The AI chat is not a secure channel for sensitive commercial communications.

For sensitive inquiries, please contact us directly by phone or email as described in Section 7. All AI chat data handling is subject to our Privacy Policy.
