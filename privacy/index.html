1. Who We Are

Oil Rigs Now, LLC ("Oil Rigs Now," "we," "us," or "our") is an independent commercial brokerage and marketing company. We are consultants in the sale and sourcing of new and used land drilling rigs, mobile drilling rigs, offshore rigs and vessels, equipment, parts, and oilfield services.

Registered Office: Houston, TX 77040, United States
Email: info@oilrigsnow.com
Phone: 713-565-0747

We operate the website at oilrigsnow.com and new.oilrigsnow.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle personal data collected through the Site and in the course of our business activities.

Data Controller For purposes of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), Oil Rigs Now, LLC acts as the data controller for personal data collected through this website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact inquiries: Name, email address, phone number, company name, country, and message content when you submit a contact form or request for quotation (RFQ).
  • Equipment inquiries: Information about the equipment you are seeking to buy or sell, including specifications, quantity, budget range, and timeline.
  • AI chat conversations: Text you enter in our AI-powered chat assistant (see Section 5 below).
  • Email correspondence: Content of emails you send to our addresses.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, links clicked, time spent on pages, referring URL, search queries on the Site.
  • Device & browser data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device type, screen resolution.
  • Log data: Server access logs including request timestamps, HTTP status codes, and bytes transferred.
  • Cookies and local storage: Session identifiers, language preferences, consent records (see Section 4).

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Analytics data from providers such as Cloudflare Analytics or Google Analytics.
  • If you interact with us through LinkedIn or other professional networks, publicly available profile information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Respond to inquiries: To reply to your RFQs, contact forms, and equipment sourcing requests.
  • Provide our services: To facilitate equipment sales, sourcing, financing consultations, and related transactions.
  • Site operations: To maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the Site and its features.
  • Analytics: To understand how visitors use our Site and improve user experience.
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and court orders.
  • Language preferences: To remember your preferred display language across visits.
  • Cookie consent: To record and honor your privacy preferences.

We do not sell your personal data, use it for advertising profiling, or share it with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Legal Bases (GDPR)

  • Contract performance: Processing necessary to respond to your inquiry or fulfill a transaction you initiated.
  • Legitimate interests: Site security, analytics, and fraud prevention — balanced against your privacy interests.
  • Legal obligation: Compliance with applicable law (export controls, sanctions, tax records).
  • Consent: Non-essential cookies and AI chat data collection where we request and record your agreement.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our Site uses cookies and similar technologies. You can review our full Cookie Policy for details. In summary:

Essential Cookies

Required for the Site to function. These include your language preference (stored in localStorage) and cookie consent status. No opt-out required — these are set automatically.

Analytics Cookies

We use Cloudflare Analytics (or equivalent) to understand aggregate Site usage. These do not track individual users across other websites and do not use advertising identifiers. Set only with your consent.

AI Chat Cookies

Our AI chat widget may use session cookies to maintain conversation context and may log conversation transcripts (see Section 5). Set only with your consent.

Managing Cookies

You may withdraw cookie consent at any time by clicking "Cookie Settings" in the footer. You can also manage cookies through your browser settings — note that disabling essential cookies may affect Site functionality.

5. AI Chat Data

Our Site features an AI-powered chat assistant. Please read our AI Chat Disclosure for full details. Key points:

  • Conversations with the AI assistant may be stored and reviewed by Oil Rigs Now staff to improve service quality and ensure accuracy.
  • Do not share sensitive personal, financial, or confidential information in the chat window.
  • The AI assistant does not create binding agreements. Any quotes, prices, or commitments expressed in chat are indicative only and must be confirmed in writing by an Oil Rigs Now representative.
  • Chat conversation data is retained for up to 24 months, then deleted.
  • You may request deletion of your chat history by emailing info@oilrigsnow.com with "Chat Data Deletion Request" in the subject line.
Transparency The chat interface includes a visible indicator that you are communicating with an AI, not a human. Human escalation is available upon request.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Cloudflare Pages / Cloudflare Analytics: Website hosting and privacy-respecting analytics. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts: Web fonts loaded from Google's CDN. Google may collect usage data. Google Privacy Policy
  • GitHub: Our source code repository. Content deployments run through GitHub. GitHub Privacy Statement
  • AI Chat Provider: Our AI assistant is powered by a third-party AI platform. Conversation data is processed according to that provider's data processing agreement with us. We will update this section when the specific provider is disclosed.

We do not embed Facebook Pixel, Google Ads, or other advertising trackers on our Site.

7. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We share your personal data only in the following circumstances:

  • Within our group: Between Oil Rigs Now, LLC for business operations, subject to equivalent protections.
  • Service providers: Vendors who process data on our behalf (hosting, analytics, AI infrastructure) under written data processing agreements that restrict use to our instructions.
  • Business transactions: Equipment buyers or sellers in a transaction you have initiated, limited to information necessary to complete the transaction.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, export regulations, or governmental authority — including U.S., EU, Canadian, and international compliance obligations.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the successor entity under equivalent privacy protections.
  • Protection of rights: To enforce our Terms of Service, protect the safety of our users, or prevent fraud or illegal activity.

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy:

  • Inquiry and RFQ records: 5 years from last contact (for business record-keeping and legal compliance).
  • Transaction records: 7 years (tax and financial compliance obligations).
  • AI chat conversations: 24 months.
  • Analytics data: Aggregated data indefinitely; any personally identifiable analytics data for 14 months.
  • Cookie consent records: 3 years.
  • Server logs: 90 days.

When data is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it. You may request early deletion — see Section 10 (Your Rights).

9. International Data Transfers

Oil Rigs Now operates from the United States. Our servers and service providers may be located in the US and other countries. If you are located outside the US — particularly in the EU/EEA, United Kingdom, or Canada — your data will be transferred internationally.

EU/EEA and UK Transfers

We transfer personal data from the EU/EEA and UK to the United States under one or more of the following mechanisms:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs): EU-approved contractual protections with our service providers.
  • Adequacy decisions: Where the destination country has been deemed adequate by the European Commission.
  • Your explicit consent: When you submit an inquiry from the EU/EEA or UK, you consent to the transfer necessary to respond.

International Context

We serve clients globally including in the Middle East, Asia, Africa, and Latin America. We are committed to applying appropriate safeguards regardless of where data originates. Our compliance program addresses applicable international data protection requirements.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have some or all of the following rights regarding your personal data. To exercise any right, email info@oilrigsnow.com with subject "Privacy Rights Request" — we will respond within 30 days (or sooner as required by law).

Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Correction

Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

Deletion ("Right to be Forgotten")

Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.

Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (EU/CA residents).

Restriction

Ask us to limit processing of your data in certain circumstances.

Objection

Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

Withdraw Consent

Revoke any consent you have given, at any time, without affecting past processing.

Complaint

Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

We will not discriminate against you for exercising any privacy right.

11. GDPR — EU & EEA Residents

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) provides you with specific rights (enumerated in Section 10 above) and requires us to provide additional disclosures.

Legal Bases for Processing

All processing of EU/EEA personal data is conducted under a valid legal basis under Article 6 GDPR:

  • Art. 6(1)(a) — Consent: For non-essential cookies and AI chat data.
  • Art. 6(1)(b) — Contract: To respond to your RFQ or business inquiry.
  • Art. 6(1)(c) — Legal obligation: Tax, export control, and sanctions compliance.
  • Art. 6(1)(f) — Legitimate interests: Site security, fraud prevention, analytics.

Supervisory Authority

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority. A list of EU supervisory authorities is available at edpb.europa.eu. UK residents may contact the ICO (ico.org.uk).

EU Representative

At this time, Oil Rigs Now, LLC does not have a formal EU representative appointed under Article 27 GDPR. EU/EEA data subjects may contact us directly at info@oilrigsnow.com for all privacy matters. We are evaluating whether Article 27 representation is required based on our processing volume.

12. CCPA/CPRA — California Residents

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), grants you specific rights.

Categories of Personal Information Collected

In the preceding 12 months, we have collected the following categories of personal information:

  • Identifiers (name, email, IP address)
  • Commercial information (equipment inquiry details, transaction history)
  • Internet or network activity (pages visited, clicks, device data)
  • Geolocation data (country/region inferred from IP address)
  • Inferences (language preference)

California Rights

  • Right to Know: Request details about what personal information we collect, use, share, or sell.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to exceptions.
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing: We do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any CCPA right.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: We do not process sensitive personal information beyond what is required for business operations.

To submit a California privacy request, email info@oilrigsnow.com with subject "California Privacy Request." We will respond within 45 days, with a possible 45-day extension.

Do Not Sell or Share

We do not sell or share (for cross-context behavioral advertising) any personal information. No opt-out action is required, but you may confirm this by contacting us.

Shine the Light: California Civil Code § 1798.83 permits California residents to request information about personal information disclosed to third parties for direct marketing. We do not disclose personal information for direct marketing purposes.

13. PIPEDA — Canadian Residents

If you are a Canadian resident, the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable provincial privacy laws govern our handling of your personal information.

PIPEDA Principles We Follow

  • Accountability: We are responsible for personal information under our control. Our President is our Privacy Officer, reachable at info@oilrigsnow.com.
  • Identifying purposes: We identify purposes for collection at or before the time of collection.
  • Consent: We obtain meaningful consent for collection, use, and disclosure, except where law permits otherwise.
  • Limiting collection: We collect only the information necessary for identified purposes.
  • Limiting use, disclosure, and retention: We use information only for identified purposes and retain it only as long as necessary.
  • Accuracy: We keep personal information as accurate, complete, and up-to-date as necessary.
  • Safeguards: We protect personal information with appropriate security measures.
  • Openness: We make our privacy practices readily available (this Policy).
  • Individual access: Upon request, we will inform individuals about the personal information we hold and allow corrections.
  • Challenging compliance: Individuals may challenge our compliance with PIPEDA by contacting us or the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada.

14. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit (TLS 1.2+)
  • Access controls limiting data access to personnel with a business need
  • Regular review of our data handling practices
  • Vendor due diligence for third-party service providers

No internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you suspect a security breach affecting your data, please contact us immediately at info@oilrigsnow.com.

In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and relevant authorities as required by applicable law (GDPR Article 33/34, PIPEDA, and applicable US state laws).

15. Children's Privacy

Our Site and services are directed exclusively at business professionals in the oil and gas industry. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 16. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a minor, we will delete it promptly. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, please contact us at info@oilrigsnow.com.

Previous versions of this Policy are available upon request by emailing info@oilrigsnow.com.
