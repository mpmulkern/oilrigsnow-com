Who We Are

Trusted Consultants Since 1985

Oil Rigs Now, LLC is a specialist consultant in the sale and sourcing of new and used drilling rigs and oilfield equipment worldwide. Based in Houston, Texas, we connect serious buyers and motivated sellers across land, mobile, and offshore markets — from single workover units to multi-rig fleet transactions.

Our inventory spans land drilling rigs, mobile and trailer-mounted rigs, jackup platforms, semi-submersibles, barge rigs, and a broad range of oilfield equipment including tubular goods, production equipment, BOPs, top drives, and power systems. We also source and broker individual parts and components for operators looking to minimize downtime.

Beyond brokerage, we assist with drilling rig financing — connecting buyers with lenders who understand the unique needs of oilfield asset acquisition. Whether you are acquiring a single rig for a new contract or liquidating a fleet, our team brings decades of market knowledge and industry relationships to every transaction.
