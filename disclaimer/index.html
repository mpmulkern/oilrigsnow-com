Oil Rigs Now, LLC makes no representation or warranty, express or implied, as to the accuracy, completeness, or currentness of any specification.

  • Contain typographical errors or transcription errors

  • Be incomplete — not all components may be listed or described

Independent verification of all specifications is required before purchase. We strongly recommend engaging qualified oilfield equipment inspectors to physically inspect any equipment.

All equipment is sold "as-is, where-is" unless a separate written sales agreement expressly provides otherwise. Oil Rigs Now makes no warranty of merchantability, fitness for a particular purpose, or freedom from defects with respect to any equipment listed on this site.

  • Be stock or representative images, not photographs of the specific unit for sale

Where photographs are representative or archival, we endeavor to note this in the listing. Buyers should request current photographs and inspection reports prior to committing to a purchase.
