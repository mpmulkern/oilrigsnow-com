2011 Drillmec MR-8000 semitrailer-mounted mechanical rig with 440,000 lb hook load and dual 1000 HP mud pumps.

The 2011 Drillmec MR-8000 is a 1000 HP semitrailer-mounted mechanical fast-moving land rig. The Drillmec mast delivers 440,000 lb static hook load with 10 lines at 104 ft clear height. Power comes from two CAT C-15 engines (1,080 HP combined for drawworks), two CAT 3512 DITA engines for mud pumps, and three CAT C15s for AC services. Dual Drillmec 9-T1000 mud pumps at 1,000 HP each handle drilling fluid. Includes BOP (13-5/8" × 5,000 psi), drill string, and a 100-man site camp. Available through Total Drilling Supply LLC.
