Deepwater Moored Harsh-Environment Semisubmersible — Enhanced Victory Class, Upgraded 2020

The Ocean Onyx is a harsh-environment moored semisubmersible built to the Enhanced Victory Class design with major upgrade completed 2020. Designed for 6,000ft water depth and 30,000ft drilling depth, she carries an NOV TDS-8SA top drive, four 900hp NOV mud pumps, and a 12-point mooring system. Accommodation for 140 personnel.
