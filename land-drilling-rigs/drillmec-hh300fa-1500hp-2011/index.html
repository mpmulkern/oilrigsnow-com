Drillmec HH-300FA hydraulic single-lift ram rig — 1,500 HP, recertified 2022, with full pipe handling automation.

Drillmec HH-300FA hydraulic single-lift ram rig built 2011 and recertified 2022. Rated 1,500 HP with 600,000-lb hook load at 10 lines and 14,765-ft design depth. Features Drillmec HH275 top drive (600K lb capacity, 36,200 ft-lb torque), full-auto pipe handling and make-up system, two Drillmec 12-T-160 triplex mud pumps at 1,600 HP each, and three MTU 12V4000 gensets at 1,500 HP each. MI Swaco Mongoose shale shakers and 1,344-bbl active mud system.
