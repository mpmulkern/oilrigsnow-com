High-capacity BOMCO 2000 HP land rig — new build to KOC specification with 2,200 HP pumps and 15K BOP package.

New-build 2000 HP BOMCO land drilling rig engineered to Kuwait Oil Company specification with 2,200 HP mud pumps and a full 15K BOP package. The JJ450/46-K front-opening K-type mast delivers a 4,500 kN (1,011,640 lb) hook load at 12 lines with 152.5 feet of clear working height. Explosion-proof CCTV and comprehensive fall-protection systems are standard throughout.

Capabilities

Key Advantages

01

High-Capacity Hoisting

The BOMCO JJ450/46-K mast delivers a 4,500 kN (1,011,640 lb) static hook load at 12 lines with 152.5 feet of clear working height. The structure meets API 4F standards with wind survival ratings of 75 knots unloaded and 32 knots under full operating load.

02

15K BOP Well Control

15,000 PSI BOP package with hydraulic 80-ton hoist system and unobstructed substructure designed for full BOP stack mobility. Subbase is free of cross-bracing hindering BOP movement, with BOP secondary retention system installed.

03

KOC-Spec Engineering

Engineered to Kuwait Oil Company specification with hook load capacity verification per KOC-2000HP-DOC-001. 2,200 HP mud pump package included. Explosion-proof CCTV and safety systems throughout to API RP 500/505 Class 1, Zone 1 & 2 requirements.
