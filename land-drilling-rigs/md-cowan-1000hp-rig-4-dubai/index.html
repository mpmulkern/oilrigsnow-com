Super Single - AC-SCR-DC - Dubai Located - Ready for Mobilisation

MD Cowan Rig #4 is a 1,000 HP DC Super Single drilling unit located in Dubai, built to MD525-E specifications. The rig features a Rig Tech RT-400BE drawworks, a 72-foot IDS mast rated at 550,000 lbs hook load, a Venture Tech XK-250 hydraulic top drive, triple CAT 3512B prime movers with KATO generators, dual Weatherford MP-13 1,300 HP mud pumps, and a complete well control and solids system. Mobilised on 15 trailers.

Capabilities

Key Advantages

01

Super Single Configuration

MD525-E Super Single design optimises footprint for single-well operations, with 15 trailers for efficient rigging and mobilisation. Rated for 12,000 ft nominal drilling depth.

02

Venture Tech Hydraulic Top Drive

The XK-250 hydraulic top drive with 500,000 lb capacity and 165 RPM max speed provides flexible rotational control without AC electrical requirements, ideal for remote or power-limited locations.

03

Dual Weatherford Mud Pumps

Two Weatherford MP-13 mud pumps at 1,300 HP each, backed by a comprehensive mud system totalling over 2,900 BBL across water, reserve, active, and treatment tanks.

04

Complete Solids Control

Two Derrick Flo-Line Cleaner 504 shale shakers, three 10-inch desander cones, twelve 4-inch desilter cones, Derrick vacuum degasser, and a mud gas separator ensure optimal fluid quality.

05

Dubai Located - Ready to Move

Currently positioned in Dubai for rapid deployment to Middle East, Asia Pacific, or African drilling programs. All 15 trailers included in the sale package.

06

AC-SCR-DC Power Plant

Triple CAT 3512B engines driving KATO 1,365 KW generators through a 4-bay Omron Ross Hill SCR system provide reliable power for demanding drilling operations.
