6th-Gen Ultra-Deepwater Harsh Environment Semisubmersible — 9,840ft WD

The Ocean Greatwhite is a sixth-generation ultra-deepwater harsh-environment semisubmersible built to the Moss CS60E design, entered service 2016. Rated to 9,840ft water depth and 35,000ft drilling depth, she carries an NOV TDX-1000 top drive, four 2,200hp NOV mud pumps, and an 8-thruster DP system. Accommodation for 180 personnel.
