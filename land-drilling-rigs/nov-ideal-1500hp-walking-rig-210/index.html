NOV Ideal Rig #210 is a refurbished 1,500 HP walking drilling system, the sister rig to Rig #209, located in Houston, Texas. Featuring the NOV JC-50-D drawworks, a 142-foot hydraulic-raised mast, VARCO TDS-11 top drive, and a Columbia Industries bi-directional walking system, this rig also boasts triple KATO/CAT 3512-B generators, dual 1,600-HP Bomco mud pumps, and a complete solids control package.

Capabilities

Key Advantages

01

Bi-Directional Walking

Columbia Industries bi-directional rig walking system enables pad-drilling efficiency through rapid lateral moves without full rig teardown, ideal for multi-well pad operations.

02

750,000 lb Hook Load

The NOV Ideal 142-foot two-section hydraulic-raised mast delivers 750,000 lb static hook load capacity with fall arrest and climb assist for safe operations.

03

Dual 1,600 HP Mud Pumps

Twin Bomco 1,600-HP triplex mud pumps provide heavy-duty circulation. Each pump driven by two YONGJI YZ08 electric traction motors with a full suction and discharge manifold.

04

Complete Solids Control

Two DERRICK FLC-514 linear motion shale shakers, NOV desander with three 10-inch cones, and a vertical desilter with twenty 4-inch cones ensure clean mud returns.

05

Refurbished & Houston Located

Rig #210 has been professionally refurbished and is ready for inspection or mobilization from Houston, TX, reducing transit time to US land drilling locations.

06

VARCO TDS-11 Top Drive

500-ton VARCO TDS-11 top drive provides reliable rotational control for directional and vertical well programs, backed by a full set of NOV traveling equipment.
