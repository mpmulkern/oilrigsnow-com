MD Cowan Super Single — 1000HP AC-SCR-DC Land Drilling Rig

MD Cowan MD525-E Super Single 1000HP DC drilling rig rated to 12,000ft depth. Features IDS 72ft mast, Rig Tech RT-400BE drawworks, Venture Tech XK-250 hydraulic top drive, 3×CAT 3512B engines driving through Omron Ross Hill SCR, twin Weatherford MP-13 mud pumps, and full BOP package. Compact 5-trailer package from Oil Rig Leasing Inc., Houston.

Capabilities

Key Advantages

01

Compact 5-Trailer Package

ORL Rig 5 ships as a complete 5-trailer package out of Houston — smaller footprint than Rig 4 while retaining the full MD Cowan Super Single specification including IDS mast, top drive, mud system, and well control.

02

AC-SCR-DC Drive Train

Three Caterpillar 3512B prime movers feed through KATO 1950 KVA generators and a 4-bay Omron/Ross Hill SCR system, providing reliable DC traction power at 600V across all rig functions.

03

Full Solids Control Suite

Twin Weatherford MP-13 1300HP mud pumps, 2390 bbl total tank capacity, and a complete Derrick solids-control train with de-sander, de-silter, vacuum degasser, and mud-gas separator.
