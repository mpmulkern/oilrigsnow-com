2000 HP Dreco/Emsco triple electric rig with NOV TDS-11SA top drive, refurbished 2014, stacked in Georgia.

Rig 425 is a 2,000 HP triple electric land drilling rig built in 1981 and refurbished in 2014. Features a 142-ft Dreco mast rated at 1,000,000 lb static hook load, Emsco C-2 drawworks, NOV TDS-11SA 500-ton top drive, and twin American Block K-1600 mud pumps. Rated to 25,000 ft vertical depth. Currently stacked and available in Georgia, USA.
