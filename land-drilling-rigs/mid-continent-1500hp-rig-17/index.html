Fully equipped 1,500 HP AC SCR land rig with triple mud pumps and Canrig top drive — ready for demanding operations.

Purpose-built 1,500 HP AC land drilling rig with Mid-Continent 914 drawworks, triple 1,600 HP FB 1600 mud pumps, and a Canrig 1250AC 500-ton top drive rated at 7,500 PSI. The DSI 90-foot skid system features wireless control, Caterpillar 3512C generator sets, and a fully integrated 1,100 BBL mud system — a high-spec package built for extended reach and demanding well programs.

Capabilities

Key Advantages

01

Triple-Pump Hydraulic Power

Three 1,600 HP FB 1600 mud pumps with dual 1,000 HP GE 752 motors each deliver 7,500 PSI fluid pressure — exceptional flow and pressure headroom for extended-reach and deep well programs.

02

500-Ton AC Top Drive

Canrig 1250AC top drive rated at 500 tons and 7,500 PSI, with rig-floor mounted TDSU and hydraulic catwalk, enables continuous drill-string rotation and rapid pipe handling with minimal crew exposure.

03

Wireless Skid Mobility

90-foot skid system with wireless control and redundant HPU allows repositioning without personnel under load, cutting rig-move time and improving field safety on multi-well pad programs.
