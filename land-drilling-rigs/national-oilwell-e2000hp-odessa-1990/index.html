National OilWell E-2000 HP Diesel Electric — Certified, Odessa TX

National OilWell E-2000 HP single drum diesel electric drilling rig located in Odessa, Texas. Built in 1990 with Cat 4 certification maintained through 2024. Features a 142-ft Lee C. Moore mast rated 1,300,000-lb SHL, Canrig 500-ton top drive, and three OilWell A-1700PT triplex mud pumps. Detailed inspection report included.
