1500 HP inland barge rig with full well history — Louisiana Gulf Coast

24-hour inland bay barge rig constructed in 2002 and actively worked in Louisiana through 2021. Features National 110M drawworks (2,280 HP input), Varco TDS-11 500-ton top drive, dual Gardner Denver PZ-11 mud pumps powered by 3516 CAT engines, and 1,000 bbl mud system. Barge dimensions 195' x 54' x 12'. Available for purchase at $5M or as-is plus upgrade package to 2,000 or 3,000 HP.
