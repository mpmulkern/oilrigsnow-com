Brewster N4-A 750HP — certified 2024, lease-to-own, two F-1000 triplex pumps

Brewster N4-A 750HP mechanical drilling rig, certified in 2024 and available on a lease-to-own basis at $1.5M down plus $7,500 per day. Critical components covered by the lessor. Features a 105 ft Superior Derrick with 300,000 lb hook load, two Caterpillar C-13 engines (830 HP total), dual Emsco F-1000 triplex mud pumps, and a full solids-control system. Drill pipe and BOP included.
