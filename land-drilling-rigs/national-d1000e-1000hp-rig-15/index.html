1,000 HP National D1000E SCR rig with hydraulic walking system and Warrior top drive — fully equipped and ready to drill.

A well-appointed 1,000 HP National D1000E land rig featuring a DSII 142' triple derrick rated at 550,000# static hook load, triple Emsco F-1000 mud pumps, dual Caterpillar 3512C generators through a Ross Hill SCR house, and a 250-ton Warrior top drive. The hydraulic walking system and complete solids-control train make this rig ready for immediate deployment.

Capabilities

Key Advantages

01

SCR DC Drive System

Dual Caterpillar 3512C engines paired with a Ross Hill 3-Bay SCR house deliver reliable, variable-speed DC power through 752-motor drawworks and Emsco triplex pumps — a proven configuration for extended drilling campaigns.

02

Hydraulic Walking

Factory hydraulic walking system eliminates costly rig move time between pad locations, reducing non-productive time and maximizing operational efficiency on multi-well pad programs.

03

Full Solids Control Train

Complete Brandt desander/desilter, dual Derrick 504 linear-motion shakers, Derrick Drilco degasser, and a closed-loop 560-barrel mud system support continuous drilling without interruption.
