Compact CAT-powered truck-mounted ZJ30 drilling/workover rig — rated to 3,000m, ready for Iraq operations.

A 750 HP (550 kW) ZJ30 series truck-mounted drilling and workover land rig currently located in Iraq. Powered by a Caterpillar C15 engine, the rig is rated to 3,000 metres on 4.5-inch drill pipe with a maximum static hook load of 1,800 kN. Features a two-section telescoping mast at 38 metres, full BOP stack, mud circulation system with shale shakers and degasser, AC electrical system, and complete auxiliary packages. All-in-one transport design suits remote locations and fast mobilisation.

Capabilities

Key Advantages

01

Self-Contained Truck-Mount

All major components — engine, drawworks, mast, rotary table, and hydraulics — are mounted on a purpose-built carrier frame. Fast mobilisation and minimal crane work required on location.

02

Full Mud Circulation System

Dedicated pump unit with triplex mud pump, high-pressure manifold, shale shakers, desander, desilter, degasser, and five mud tank stages handle demanding Iraq formation requirements.

03

Complete BOP & Well Control

Full API-compliant well-control package includes annular BOP, double ram BOP, hydraulic accumulator BOP control system, choke manifold, kill manifold, and IBOP.
