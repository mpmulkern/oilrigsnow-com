1,500 HP Davis 1500-E SCR rig with walking system, 350-ton top drive, and triple Cat 3512C power.

Rig 11 is a complete 1,500 HP Davis 1500-E drilling package powered by three Caterpillar 3512C generators (1,365 kW each) through a Ross Hill 3×4 bay SCR house. The 142-foot Superior cantilever mast carries a 768,000-pound static hook load and is fitted with a 350-ton Warrior hydraulic top drive and Columbia In-Sub walking system. Twin 3-F-1600 triplex pumps deliver 7,500 psi, and a comprehensive BOP stack with 13⅝" annular and dual ram preventers rounds out this capable land drilling package.

Capabilities

Key Advantages

01

Triple Cat 3512C SCR Power

Three Caterpillar 3512C generators at 1,365 kW each feed a Ross Hill 3×4 bay SCR house, delivering stable DC power to the Davis 1500-E drawworks and twin 3-F-1600 triplex pumps at 7,500 psi.

02

Walking System Ready

Columbia In-Sub Walking System with HPU enables pad drilling without rigging down — a competitive capability on multi-well pad locations.

03

350-Ton Top Drive

The 350-ton Warrior hydraulic top drive with 7,500 psi circulation rating adds directional drilling capability and reduces connection time compared to conventional kelly systems.
