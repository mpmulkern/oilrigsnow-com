1000HP carrier-mounted drilling rig — mobile platform built for Latin American field operations.

The LOADCRAFT LCI-1000 is a 1000HP carrier-integrated drilling rig designed for mobility in demanding field conditions. Featuring a 114-ft mast with 365,000-lb hook load, dual 1000HP MAXUM triplex pumps, and a WARRIOR 250-ton top drive, this rig offers serious capability in a compact, road-transportable package. Currently configured and operated in Brazil.
