Capabilities

Key Advantages

01

AC-VFD Power Architecture

Five CAT C-3512B generators feeding a 6,500 kW Drillmec VFD power control room deliver smooth, variable-frequency drive control across all rotating equipment — top drive, draw works, and mud pumps.

02

Triple Drillmec Mud Pumps

Three Drillmec 12T1600 pumps each driven by twin GE B22 AC 1,150 HP motors, rated to 5,000 psi, with a 2,282-bbl total mud system and Mi-Swaco solids-control train.

03

Complete Camp & Logistics

132-person main camp plus 12-person mini camp, Manitou and Terex material handling, and full slickline capability make this rig self-sufficient for remote or international operations.
