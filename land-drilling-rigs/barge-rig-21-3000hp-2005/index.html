TDS Barge Rig 21 — 3,000 HP Inland Barge Rig, Constructed 2005

TDS Barge Drilling Rig 21 is a 24-hour inland bay barge drilling rig constructed in 2005, rated to 30,000 feet with a 2,000,000-lb hook load. Powered by Continental EMSCO C-3 Type 2 drawworks and a Canrig 750-ton top drive, Rig 21 is equipped for deep-shelf barge drilling in the Gulf Coast transition zone and shallow water environments worldwide.

Capabilities

Key Advantages

01

Deep Drilling Capability

SOMAC 152-ft mast with 2,000,000-lb static hook load strung on 16 lines. Rated to 30,000 ft with 5.5" HT-55 drill pipe and 15/8" – 8" drill collars. BOP stack rated to 10,000 PSI.

02

Shallow Water Access

Floating hull designed for inland bays and transition zone environments with 8-ft towing draft. Capable of accessing very shallow water locations (7–9 ft) in Gulf Coast marshes and bayous.

03

BOP & Well Control

Annular 13 5/8" 5,000 PSI rams, 13 5/8" 10,000 PSI drilling spool, 2-10,000 PSI HCR valves, and 2-10,000 PSI manual valves. 2-50 ton air-operated and manual chain falls for BOP handling.
