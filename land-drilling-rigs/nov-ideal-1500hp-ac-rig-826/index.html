AC VFD Drilling System • 1,500 HP • 18,000 ft Depth Rating • Available Now

NOV Ideal Rig #826 is a 1,500 HP AC variable frequency drive drilling system rated to 18,000 ft. Equipped with the NOV SSGD-360 drawworks, a 142-foot cantilever mast with 720,000 lb hook load, NOV TDS-11SA top drive, and dual NOV 12-P-160 mud pumps at 1,600 HP each, this VFD rig offers full electronic driller control with a Sling Shot substructure and comprehensive solids control package.

Capabilities

Key Advantages

01

AC VFD Drive System

Full AC variable frequency drive architecture provides smooth, precise control of drawworks, top drive, and mud pumps. The VFD system reduces mechanical wear and enables advanced drilling automation.

02

18,000 ft Depth Rating

Rated to 18,000 ft drilling depth with 20,000 ft of mast racking capacity, this rig handles deep vertical and directional wells with 80 estimated move loads per year.

03

NOV TDS-11SA Top Drive

The 500-ton NOV TDS-11SA delivers 37,500 ft-lb continuous torque at 125 RPM. Combined with the NOV ZP-375 rotary table and ST-80 CL iron roughneck, tubular handling is fully mechanized.

04

Dual NOV 12-P-160 Mud Pumps

Two NOV 12-P-160 pumps rated at 1,600 HP each and 5,000 PSI pressure supply robust mud circulation. Active system holds 792 BBL; reserve system holds 402 BBL.

05

Complete BOP Stack

Well control equipment includes a Shaffer 13-5/8-inch 5,000 PSI spherical annular, a Shaffer 10,000 PSI LXT single ram, MI Swaco choke manifold, and Sara Sae closing unit.

06

Sling Shot Substructure

The 25-foot Sling Shot substructure with 21-foot clear height and 750,000 lb casing capacity supports BOP handling and accommodates large-diameter wellhead equipment.
