3300HP AC DBS Drilling Rig — Ultra-Deep Desert Spec, Kuwait

A high-capacity 3300HP AC DBS drilling rig engineered for extreme deep-well desert operations in Kuwait. Features a 160-foot K-style cantilever mast, 1,700,000-lb static hook load, three quintuplex 7,500 PSI mud pumps, seven CAT 3512CHD generators totaling 8,330 ekW, and a 1,000-ton AC top drive. Desert-rated to +55°C ambient with full instrumentation and CCTV.
