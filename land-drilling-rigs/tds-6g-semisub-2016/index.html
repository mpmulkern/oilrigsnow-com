6th-generation DP/moored ultra-deepwater semisub, 9,840 ft WD, 5,750 HP drawworks, 8x azimuth thrusters.

A sixth-generation ultra-deepwater semisubmersible on the Moss CS60E design, delivered in 2016. Rated to 9,840 ft water depth with DP and mooring capability, the rig features a 5,750 HP NOV SSGD drawworks, NOV TDX-1000 1,000-ton top drive, four 2,200 HP mud pumps at 7,500 psi, and eight 4,800 kW Thrustmaster azimuth thrusters. DnV classified with 180-person accommodation and a triple-pit segregated mud system for simultaneous handling of WBM, NAF, and brine.

Capabilities

Key Advantages

01

9,840 ft Rated DP Platform

Kongsberg K-POS DP system with 8x 4,800 kW Thrustmaster azimuth thrusters enables full dynamic positioning to 9,840 ft water depth — or switch to 8-point Rolls-Royce mooring for extended campaigns.

02

5,750 HP Drawworks

The NOV SSGD drawworks delivers 5,750 HP for efficient tripping on ultra-deep wells. Paired with the NOV TDX-1000 top drive (1,000 ST, 91,000 ft-lbs at 115 rpm) and passive/active heave compensation.

03

Triple-Pit Segregated Mud System

Simultaneously handles three fluid types — WBM, NAF, and brine — with 15,051 bbl total liquid mud storage, 6x NOV VSM Multisizer shakers, and four 2,200 HP mud pumps at 7,500 psi.

04

6th-Gen Dual-Derrick-Ready Design

2x NOV HR IV Rackers, 2x NOV MPT Iron Roughnecks, and a powered mousehole enable efficient dual-activity operations, maximizing time on bottom in ultra-deepwater environments.
