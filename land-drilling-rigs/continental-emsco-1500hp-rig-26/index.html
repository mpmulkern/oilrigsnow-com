1500 HP Continental Emsco C1 SCR electric land rig with DSI 142-ft mast — fully equipped with Canrig top drive, catwalk, and complete solids-control package.

Continental Emsco C1 1500 HP SCR electric land drilling rig featuring a DSI 142-ft mast rated to 750,000 lb static hook load and a 26.5-ft floor height substructure. Power comes from three Caterpillar 3512C diesel engines with 1,365 KW generators. The rig includes a Canrig 1250AC 500-ton top drive, dual 1600 HP mud pumps rated to 7,500 PSI, a 1,100 BBL mud system with full solids control, and a Canrig PC-3000 hydraulic catwalk with walking package.

Capabilities

Key Advantages

01

High-Capacity AC Electric Drive

The Continental Emsco C1 drawworks powered by dual GE 752 motors delivers precise SCR-controlled 1500 HP torque, ideal for deep formations and challenging drilling programs requiring consistent power management.

02

Heavy-Lift DSI Mast

The DSI 142-ft mast rated to 750,000 lb static hook load accommodates large bottom-hole assemblies and heavy casing strings. The Canrig 1250AC 500-ton top drive provides full directional drilling capability.

03

Complete Surface Equipment

Dual 1600 HP mud pumps at 7,500 PSI, 1,100 BBL pit system with full solids control (desander, desilter, mud/gas separator), and Canrig PC-3000 hydraulic catwalk with walking package provide a fully integrated rig package.
