MD Cowan Super Single AC-SCR-DC — compact 1000HP drilling unit on 15 trailers

MD Cowan Super Single Rig 3 (MD525-E) is a DC-driven 1000HP drilling unit rated to 12,000 ft nominal depth. Powered by three CAT 3512B prime movers driving Kato 1950kVA/1365kW generators through an AC-SCR-DC system, it features a Rig Tech RT-400BE 1000HP drawworks, IDS 72-foot mast with 550,000 lb hook load, Venture Tech XK-250 hydraulic top drive, and two Weatherford MP-13 1300HP mud pumps. Complete rig ships on 15 trailers. Listed by Oil Rig Leasing Inc., Houston TX.

Key Advantages

01

Super Single Compact Design

MD Cowan Super Single configuration minimizes trailer count to 15 loads for rapid mobilization to tight locations — purpose-built for onshore single-well pad and workhorse shallow-to-medium depth programs.

02

Complete Mud Treatment System

Full solids-control train including 2× Derrick shale shakers, 3×10" desanders, 12×4" desilters, mud-gas separator, and vacuum degasser provides turnkey well-control capability.

03

Hydraulic Top Drive

Venture Tech XK-250 hydraulic top drive rated 500,000 lb at 165 RPM delivers directional drilling capability without the electrical infrastructure required by AC top drives.
