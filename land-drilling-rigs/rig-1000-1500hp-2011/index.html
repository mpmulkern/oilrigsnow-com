1500HP AC 600K Cantilever Triple — 2011 Build with Walking System

A well-specified 2011-built 1500HP AC land rig featuring a 136-foot Hyduke mast rated 600,000 lbs, E80 AC drawworks, GE natural gas generators, and a Hyduke/Hodgson walking system with 900-ton lifting capacity. Equipped with NOV IDS-350PE top drive, RS F-1600 mud pumps, and Cameron BOP — rated to 20,000 feet depth.

Key Advantages

01

High-Load Walking System

Hyduke/Hodgson walking system lifts 900 tons on six Hillman rollers — enabling pad drilling without rig-down moves and significantly reducing well-to-well NPT.

02

Deep-Well AC Performance

1,500HP GE AC drive package with E80 drawworks and NOV 350-ton top drive supports programs to 20,000 feet depth with 4-1/2" drill pipe.

03

Natural Gas Power Package

Four GE Jenbacher 1,000kW natural gas generators reduce operating costs versus diesel alternatives while maintaining full rig power redundancy.
