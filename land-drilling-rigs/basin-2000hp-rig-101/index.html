2,000 HP Walking Rig - CANRIG AC Top Drive - 1M lb Hook Load - For Sale

Basin Drilling Rig #101 is a custom-built 2,000 HP walking land rig featuring a Pyramid 142-foot cantilever mast with 1,000,000 lb static hook load, a Gardner Denver 1500-E drawworks, and a CANRIG 1250AC-681 AC electric top drive rated at 500 tons and 51,400 ft-lb drilling torque. The OWI Stealthwalker multidirectional walking system, four Caterpillar 3512-B engines, and triple 1,600-HP HHF mud pumps make this an exceptional heavy-duty drilling package.

Capabilities

Key Advantages

01

1,000,000 lb Hook Load

The Pyramid 142-foot cantilever mast delivers a class-leading 1,000,000 lb static hook load on 12 lines, with a 30-foot box-on-box substructure providing 1,000,000 lb rotary table support and 600,000 lb setback capacity.

02

OWI Stealthwalker Multi-Directional

The OWI Stealthwalker system enables fast, multi-directional pad moves without disassembly, maximising drill time and reducing non-productive rig-move hours on multi-well pads.

03

CANRIG 1250AC High-Torque Top Drive

The CANRIG 1250AC-681 delivers 51,400 ft-lb drilling torque and 95,000 ft-lb breakout torque at up to 265 RPM. Rockit and Revit compatible, with a 7,500 PSI integrated swivel.

04

Triple 1,600 HP Mud Pumps

Three HHF-1600 triplex pumps with 7,500 PSI fluid ends and GE 752 traction motors provide exceptional circulation capacity backed by a 1,670 BBL active mud system and three Derrick Hyperpool shakers.

05

Cameron 10,000 PSI Pressure Control

Full pressure control system includes Shaffer spherical annular, dual Cameron Type U ram preventers, 10,000 PSI choke manifold, Koomey 8-station accumulator, and Oilworks BOP skidding system.

06

4x CAT 3512-B Power Plant

Four Caterpillar 3512-B engines with Kato 1,260 KW AC generators and Gulf Coast International 1,400 SCR house provide 5,040 KW of installed generation capacity for all rig loads.
