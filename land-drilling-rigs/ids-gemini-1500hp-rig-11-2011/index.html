Gemini 750 Series AC rig — commissioned 2011, upgraded 1500HP/750K in 2016 with Canrig top drive

IDS Gemini 750 Series Rig 11 (Leo) was originally commissioned September 2011 and upgraded to 1500HP/750K hook load in 2016. Powered by four Cummins KTA50 DR diesel engines with AVK WEG 1100kW generator sets, it features an IDS 1500HP GEB-28 AC drawworks, three Bomco F-1600 triplex mud pumps, and a Canrig 350-ton AC top drive with Rockit capability. Capabilities

Key Advantages

01

Rockit-Equipped Top Drive

Canrig 350-ton AC top drive with Rockit capability enables oscillation to free stuck pipe — a high-value feature that reduces non-productive time in difficult formations.

02

Four-Engine Power Train

Quad Cummins KTA50 DR engine package provides redundant power with individual air start and skid-mounted enclosures — enabling independent maintenance without full rig shutdown.

03

Bomco High-Pressure Mud System

Three Bomco F-1600 triplex pumps at 7500 PSI with GEB-29 AC traction motors deliver sustained high-pressure circulation for extended-reach and deep directional wells.
