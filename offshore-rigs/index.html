Vessel Classes

Four vessel classes

⚓

Jack-up Rigs

Self-elevating drilling units rated for 200–400 ft water depth. CP-300, Super M2, JU-2000E, CJ46, DSJ-300, CP-400 designs.

🚢

Semi-Submersibles

Floating drilling units for ultra-deepwater. TDS-5G, TDS-6G, Ocean Onyx (6,000 ft), Ocean Great White (9,840 ft) class vessels.

🛺

Drillships

Self-propelled deepwater drillships for global operations. Available on request from our supplier network.

🚤

Inland Barge Rigs

Flat-bottomed barge-mounted drilling units for inland waterways — primarily Gulf Coast and similar shallow-water environments.
