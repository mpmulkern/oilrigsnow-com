1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, to improve user experience, and to provide reporting information to site operators.

Cookies may be "session cookies," which are deleted when you close your browser, or "persistent cookies," which remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. Cookies set by the website operator are called "first-party cookies"; those set by third-party services are called "third-party cookies."

In addition to cookies, we may use similar tracking technologies such as pixel tags (also called web beacons or clear GIFs), which help us understand how visitors interact with our site. References to "cookies" in this Policy encompass all such technologies unless stated otherwise.

2. Why We Use Cookies

Oil Rigs Now, LLC uses cookies for several legitimate purposes:

  • Essential operation: Certain cookies are necessary for the website to function. Without them, key features such as security protections, language preferences, and page navigation would not work.
  • Performance and analytics: We use aggregate, anonymized data to understand how visitors find and use our site — which pages are most popular, how long visitors stay, and how they navigate between sections. This helps us improve our content and the overall user experience.
  • AI chat functionality: When you use the AI chat assistant on oilrigsnow.com, session-scoped cookies allow the AI to maintain context within a single conversation.
  • Remembering your preferences: Cookies allow us to remember choices you make (such as your preferred language) so you do not need to set them again on your next visit.

We do not use cookies for targeted advertising, remarketing, or selling your data to third parties. We do not operate any behavioral advertising or third-party social media tracking on this site.

4. Cookie Categories

Essential Cookies (Always Active)

Essential cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function and cannot be disabled without severely impacting site functionality. They include cookies that enable security features (such as bot protection), store your consent decision, and remember your language preference. We do not require your consent to set essential cookies, as they are necessary to deliver a service you have requested. However, you may instruct your browser to block them — please be aware that some parts of the site may then not function correctly.

Analytics Cookies (Consent Required)

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with oilrigsnow.com. We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is a privacy-first analytics solution that does not use fingerprinting and does not track individual visitors across sites or sessions. Data collected is aggregated and anonymized. These cookies are only set after you have accepted the cookie consent banner. You may decline these cookies without affecting your ability to use the site.

AI Chat Cookies (Consent Required)

The AI chat assistant on oilrigsnow.com uses a session cookie to maintain conversational context. This cookie is scoped to your current browser session and is deleted when you close your browser. It does not track your behavior across other websites or persist beyond the current chat session. These cookies are set when you open the chat widget, and you may decline them. Declining AI chat cookies means the chat assistant may not retain context between individual messages in the same conversation.

5. Managing Cookies via Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically block or delete cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this and other websites you visit.

To manage cookies in your browser, follow the instructions at the links below:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

For mobile browsers, consult the settings of your device or browser application. Instructions vary by operating system version and browser release. You can also use browser extensions such as Privacy Badger or uBlock Origin to manage cookies across all sites you visit.

Note on Opt-Out Tools: Certain industry opt-out tools (such as the NAI opt-out page at optout.networkadvertising.org) may not apply to the analytics solution we use, as Cloudflare Web Analytics is not a member of the NAI. Our analytics do not support behavioral targeting or cross-site tracking.

6. Withdrawing Your Consent

You may withdraw your consent to non-essential cookies at any time. To do so, click "Cookie Settings" in the footer of any page on oilrigsnow.com. This will reset your cookie consent preference and display the consent banner again, allowing you to make new selections.

Withdrawing consent does not affect the lawfulness of any cookie processing that occurred before you withdrew your consent. Essential cookies will continue to function regardless of your consent choice, as they are required for the site to operate.

If you have withdrawn consent and wish to ensure all non-essential cookies have been removed from your device, you should also clear your browser's cookie storage for oilrigsnow.com using the browser settings described in Section 5 above.

7. About the Cookie Consent Banner

When you visit oilrigsnow.com for the first time, or after clearing your browser cookies, a cookie consent banner is displayed at the bottom of the page. This banner:

  • Informs you that we use cookies and describes the categories of cookies in use.
  • Provides a link to this Cookie Policy for full details.
  • Offers you the ability to accept all cookies (including analytics and AI chat cookies) or decline non-essential cookies.
  • Sets the cookie_consent cookie to record your decision so the banner is not shown again for 12 months.

The banner is implemented using localStorage rather than an additional cookie, minimizing the footprint of the consent mechanism itself. Essential cookies (including the Cloudflare bot management cookie) may be set before you interact with the banner, as they are strictly necessary for secure site delivery.

If you neither accept nor decline — for example, if you close the banner without clicking either button — we treat your session as not having consented to non-essential cookies, and we do not set analytics or AI chat cookies until you explicitly accept.

8. Legal Basis for Cookie Use

Where the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or the UK GDPR applies to visitors from the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, our legal basis for setting cookies is as follows:

  • Essential cookies: Set on the basis of legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) — specifically, our interest in delivering a secure, functional website — and, where applicable, as strictly necessary for a service you have requested.
  • Analytics and functional cookies: Set on the basis of consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR), provided through the cookie consent banner in accordance with Article 7 GDPR and the ePrivacy Directive (2002/58/EC, as amended).

For visitors from California, this Policy is supplemented by our Privacy Policy, which addresses your rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).

We store your consent record (the cookie_consent value) for 12 months in accordance with our obligation to demonstrate consent under GDPR Article 7(1). You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time as described in Section 6.
