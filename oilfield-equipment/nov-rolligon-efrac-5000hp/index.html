Major Systems
How It's Built

Six integrated systems make up the NOV Rolligon SF23-8151 eFrac package.

🔌
Medium Voltage Power System
E-House houses all electrical equipment for unmanned operation — 13.8 kV input, 4,500 KVA input transformer, 5,000 HP MV-VFD.
  • 13.8 kV @ 60 Hz / 211A input
  • 4,500 KVA input transformer
  • 5,000 HP Variable Frequency Drive
  • Anti-condensation heaters throughout
  • MV/LV metal barrier segregation
⚙️
SPM QEM5000 Quintuplex Pump
NOV's 5,000 HP quintuplex pump with stainless steel fluid end, mounted at the rear of the trailer with full discharge and suction manifolding.
  • 8" stroke, 10.05:1 gear reducer
  • Stainless steel valve over valve fluid end
  • Up to 5" plungers
  • 4" Fig 1502 discharge iron
  • Single 2" Fig 1502 gauge connection
🛢️
Dual-Circuit Lube System
220-gallon stainless steel reservoir with dual circuit + kidney loop, VFD-driven lube pump, plus a separate plunger grease injection system.
  • ~220-gallon SS reservoir
  • Dual circuit + kidney loop
  • Check valve, fluid filter, adjustable relief
  • Plunger grease injection — auto-engaged
  • Plunger drip pan (removable)
🎛️
Control & Shutdown System
STIMSIGHT™ MultiPump compatible digital frac control with Ethernet fibre comms, auto overpressure shutdown, and both Emergency Stop and Emergency Kill modes.
  • STIMSIGHT™ MultiPump compatibility
  • Fibre Ethernet control network
  • Maintenance mode HMI (laptop-based)
  • Overpressure inhibit auto shutdown
  • OSHA-compliant Emergency Kill mode
📡
NOV Max™ Edge Device
Delivered Max™-ready with cellular data transfer to NOV's secure cloud, condition-based monitoring, and optional subscription to Max platform applications.
  • Cellular upload to NOV secure cloud
  • Remote real-time equipment health view
  • Alerts & alarms on monitored channels
  • GPS location tracking
  • Accelerometer CBM on motor & pump
🔧
Hose Handling System
Electric hose handling system for 2-person high-pressure iron placement — stowable for transport and field operation.
  • Electric actuation
  • 2-person operable
  • Stowable for transit
  • Moves HP iron storage ↔ hookup
Ideal For
Applications

Where the NOV Rolligon eFrac unit delivers its strongest value.

⚡
Electric Frac Fleets
Designed from the ground up as an eFrac unit — medium voltage, VFD-controlled, fully compatible with STIMSIGHT™ MultiPump coordinated fleet operations.
🤖
Unmanned Operation Programmes
E-House designed as an unmanned space. Full instrumentation, remote shutdown, and CBM support remote operational models with reduced wellsite headcount.
🛣️
Pad-to-Pad Mobility
Self-contained trailer mount — road legal, DOT compliant, 59'6" × 8'6" × 13', 5-axle air-ride suspension. Designed for efficient inter-pad moves.
🌱
ESG / Low-Emissions Completions
Zero direct emissions at wellsite. Electric drive eliminates diesel engine exhaust — supporting operators' ESG commitments and site air quality requirements.
📊
Digital / Data-Driven Operations
NOV Max™ ready with CBM, accelerometers, oil particulate monitoring, and cloud connectivity — built for operators who run predictive maintenance programmes.
🏗️
High-Pressure / High-Horsepower Jobs
15,000 PSI working pressure with 5,000 HP — suited to tight oil and gas formations demanding high treating pressures, particularly in deep completions.
